Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about merging PDF files

How do I merge PDF files?

Simply drag and drop your PDF files into the upload area or click to select files from your computer. You can select multiple files at once. After uploading, click the "Merge PDFs" button to combine them into a single document.

Is there a limit to the number of PDFs I can merge?

There's no strict limit to the number of PDFs you can merge, but very large collections may take longer to process. For best performance, we recommend merging up to 20 files at a time.

What's the difference between compression levels?

The "No Compression" option preserves the original quality but results in larger files. "Medium Compression" reduces file size while maintaining good quality. "High Compression" significantly reduces file size but may slightly affect image quality.

Are my files secure?

Absolutely! All processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your computer and are never uploaded to our servers. We don't store any of your documents.

Can I rearrange the order of pages before merging?

Yes! After uploading your files, you can drag and drop them to rearrange the order. You can also click on any file to see a preview and rearrange individual pages if needed.
